What is SingFlows ? 
===
_SingFlows_ is a research project in applied mathematics funded by the french agency [ANR](https://anr.fr/) under grant ANR-18-CE40-0027. Its goal is to develop mathematical and numerical tools for fluid flows with singularities. A detailed scientific description of the project can be found [here]()
